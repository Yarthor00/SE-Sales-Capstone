# 🚀 Capstone Exercise – Lead Solutions Engineer

## **From Problem to Pipeline: Driving Business Outcomes and Closing Opportunities with AI-Powered Observability**

---

## 📅 Delivery Format

- Slides presentation (PowerPoint or similar)
   
## 🧑‍💼 Executive Presentation

Prepare a **25–30 minute executive-level presentation**:

- Customer context and core problem  
- Business impact (speak the language of executives)  
- Proposed solution (focused on outcomes)  
- Expected value  
- Clear next steps to **advance toward closing**  

---


## 📌 Overview

As part of the evaluation process for the Lead Solutions Engineer role, this capstone is designed to assess the ability to:

- Structure and qualify high-value opportunities  
- Connect technology to measurable business outcomes  
- Drive momentum toward deal progression and closure  

This exercise is not focused only on technical depth, but on demonstrating how to **translate customer challenges into a compelling, winnable business opportunity**.

---

## 🎯 Scenario Context

You will work on an enterprise customer scenario with the following challenges:

- High incidence of production issues  
- Limited end-to-end visibility  
- Strong business pressure to improve digital experience and operational efficiency  

You may **assume** a customer in industries such as **banking, retail, or telecommunications in Mexico**.

---

## 💡 1. Solution & Value Proposition

Show how you convert the problem into a **compelling, sellable solution**:

- How you position Dynatrace in the customer context  
- Direct mapping:
  - Problem → Solution → Business Outcome  
- Key use cases that accelerate value and create urgency  
- Business-focused value proposition (outcomes > features)  
- Clear impact estimation:
  - Cost savings  
  - Efficiency gains  
  - Revenue protection or growth  

---

## 🧪 2. PoV / Demo Strategy

Demonstrate a **closing-oriented mindset**:

- PoV objectives focused on validating business value  
- Clear success criteria that justify a buying decision  
- Execution plan focused on:
  - Quick wins  
  - High-impact validation points  
- Demo storyline aligned to executive priorities  
- Identification of risks that could block the deal and mitigation strategies  

---

## 📌 Final Note

This exercise is an opportunity to demonstrate your readiness to operate as a Lead Solutions Engineer.
