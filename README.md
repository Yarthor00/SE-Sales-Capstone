# SE-Sales-Capstone

As the next step in the process for the Lead Solutions Engineer position, we would like you to develop and present a capstone exercise focused on evaluating your approach to pre-sales and your ability to connect technology with business outcomes.
The objective of this exercise is not only to assess your technical knowledge, but also to understand how you structure an opportunity, how you generate value for the client, and how you contribute to advancing a deal toward closure.
